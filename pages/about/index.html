    About Me

    My name is Sean Bush, and I am a data scientist currently living in Dallas, Texas. I specialize in turning complex data sets into actionable insights, creating and maintaining machine learning systems, and communicating complex ideas in easy-to-understand ways. I have recently graduated with my Master's in data science and am currently looking for data science and data analysis roles.

    I began my career as a high school mathematics teacher after completing my Bachelor's of Science in secondary education Mathematics Education in 2022. While teaching, I developed an interesting in programming and machine learning , which grew quickly from something I enjoyed as a hobby to something I could seriously consider as a career. Data science fulfilled many of my personal interests: mathematics, programming, communication, and problem-solving. To gain experience and essential knowledge and skills, I began pursuing my Master's in data science in 2024 and graduated in 2026. I am now actively searching for fulfilling data science positions.

    I have a significant amount of experience using tools like Python: I have analyzed datasets with Pandas, visualized results with MatPlotLib and Seaborn, developed machine learning models with Scikit-Learn, and Statsmodels, and created neural networks with PyTorch and MLFlow. I have also used PostgreSQL and SQLite to create and work with databases for various projects, used Git and DVC to version-control my code and my datasets, and used Docker to create containerized projects and host servers and APIs. My core strengths lie in predictive modeling, data analysis, and communicating actionable insights. You can view different data science projects I have worked on from the Portfolio page on this website, or visit my Blog page to explore some of my writings about data science.

    I am a detail-oriented worker who takes pride in delivering high-quality work. I value environments that encourage collaboration and communication, and that provide the opportunity to engage in creative problem-solving. I am interested in roles that involve deep analytical work, where I can explore data, develop models, and deliver insights that inform real decisions. I am also open to working on long-term machine learning systems that evolve and improve over time.

    Outside of work, I am an avid reader of fiction and nonfiction books. I also enjoy playing piano, cooking, and baking. I love swimming and regularly swim for exercise. For fun, I enjoy gaming and dabbling in game development alongside spending time with friends and family.

    If you would like to contact me about professional opportunities, you can visit the Contact page to find my personal email and my LinkedIn. I look forward to hearing from you.
